About Me

Hey, I'm Adir, also known as Adirbd.

I am drawn to structure, systems that work, and the kind of coffee you grind yourself. I care about how things are built beneath the surface, and how thoughtful design creates calm, reliable outcomes.

I spend a lot of time thinking about how systems move. How pipelines flow, how infrastructure evolves, and how people interact with tools. My goal is always to reduce friction and make complex systems feel simple to operate.

Based in Tel Aviv. When I am not working in terminals, I enjoy travel, cities, and snowboarding once a year.
